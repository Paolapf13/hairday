WELCOME TO HAIR DAY!

We're a boutique hair salon nestled in the heart of Madison, Wisconsin. We specialize in cut, color, and styling, offering a wide range of services that include makeup and nails.


Known for our warm and relaxing atmosphere, Hair Day is the go-to destination for edgy hair colors and on-trend cuts.

At Hair Day, we believe self-care should feel like a treat. That’s why we’ve created an environment where you can unwind and embrace your unique style.


Whether you're looking for a fresh cut, a vibrant new color, or a polished look for a special occasion, our skilled team is here to make your Hair Day truly unforgettable.

Founded by Pria, a certified colorist with a passion for bold, custom coloring techniques. Her vision of creating a “treat yourself” space became a reality when she teamed up with Garry, a talented hairdresser.


Together, they’ve built a team of seven dedicated professionals: two stylists, three colorists, a manicurist, and a makeup artist.
